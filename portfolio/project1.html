Cube House

The final project of Foundation 1 Studio was largely based around spacial creation and projection. The process began with the construction of three planes, intersecting in a way that supports not only proportionality, but also balance. These planes were then to be placed inside a 20'x20' cube which was to become a wedding pavilion on Lake Erie. From then on, extrusion and reflection took place to develop a structure and site plan based on program.
